### ðŸŒ Create Web App

**Steps:**
1. Go to **App Services** > click **+ Create**
2. Fill in the following settings:
   - **Name**: `my-welcome-webapp-123` *(must be globally unique)*
   - **Publish**: `Code`
   - **Runtime**: `Node.js` / `PHP` / `Python`
   - **OS**: `Linux`
   - **Region**: `East US`
   - **App Service Plan**: `myAppServicePlan`
3. Click **Create**
